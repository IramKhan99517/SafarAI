TAWAF RITUAL

Tawaf is performed around the Kaaba.

Steps:
1. Start at the Black Stone.
2. Raise hand and say “Allahu Akbar”.
3. Keep the Kaaba on your left side.
4. Walk counterclockwise.
5. Complete 7 full circles.
6. Make dua and prayers during Tawaf.
7. After completion, pray 2 rak'ahs behind Maqam Ibrahim.
